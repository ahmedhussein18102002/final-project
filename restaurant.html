Nişantaşi

Nişantaşı, named after an exclusive quarter on Istanbul’s European side, is Cairo's most exciting and stylish destination for Turkish cuisine. We offer atmospheric dining in a relaxing setting that attracts people from far and wide. The experience is unlike any other, from dishes to share with family and friends to the ceremonious flourish of a sealed terracotta cooking pot cut open at your table. Our cuisine offers so much to discover and delight in. ​ Nişantaşı is brought to you by International Food Academy

Charlie's Restaurant

Inspired by the Heliopolis of the mid 1900s; a place to make time for making time. Charlie's is a nod to the godfather of Egyptian hospitality, Charles Baehler. In the mid 1900s, Cairo was buzzing with a unique melange of Egyptian and European cultural influences, much like the spirit of Heliopolis itself. Charles was a connector of experiences. And today, we celebrate the culture of leisure he brought and say: chapeau monsieur!
